about us

RAOWA Convention Center is one of the finest convention centers in Dhaka, having served thousands of wedding events since its inception. It is usually a big challenge for customers to book a spot in this center as it is booked out throughout the wedding seasons. Because of its prime location, access to parking and spacious halls, it is an extremely popular destination not just for weddings but also various corporate and institutional events.
